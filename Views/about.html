About Us

I'm Keerthana Vijekumar, a beloved dog mom. Due to my higher studies, I had to leave my pet. I really miss him, and the regret I have for being unable to take care of him deeply hits me. Having a pet is not a choice; it's a responsibility. Within our busy schedules, it's really difficult to allocate time to take care of our beloved pets. For someone who is busy and cannot be with their pets, there is a need for a platform like PawFinders to ensure their pets are in good hands and getting their needs met. That's what PawFinders is all about: making sure to provide for the needs of our beloved fur babies.
